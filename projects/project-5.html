Hakkachi Paali (Menstrual Policy Movement)

Through this movement we aim to ensure an accessible, safer & more dignified period for all. We are here to change how menstruation is viewed & treated. Through advocacy & policy change on ground to help thousands of menstruators across genders, economic background, occupation & age reclaim their menstrual rights.

Muse Foundation, Project Naveli, and Mark Your presence are coming together to change how Menstruation is looked upon, addressed, and dealt with in Mumbai.

We are putting together a Menstrual Rights Policy that looks into areas, seldom paid attention to - Sanitation & Hygiene, Open Menstrual Conversations, Menstrual Leave, Accessible Period Products, Period rights of minorities (disabled and transgender menstruators), Disposal of Sanitary Waste, and Menstrual Health.

And we are looking for people who want to bring about this change. Fill the below form if you think your profile and experience match any of the below areas and we'll get in touch.

  • Research and drafting of the policy
  • Social Media (Content planning and execution)
  • Content creation (Social Media, Press Materials)
  • Design (posters, posts, videos for online and on-ground)
  • Grassroots connect (handling on-ground activation, data collection, creating events, etc.)

Join us in making periods an accessible and safe experience for all. #MyPeriodMyRight
