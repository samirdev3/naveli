EntrepreNaari

Together changing the gender diversity equation in India for women entrepreneurs.

#EntrepreNaari is a network that offers free and easy access to resources, networking opportunities, mentorship, guidance and community support to existing and aspiring women entrepreneurs.

The need for a network like EntrepreNaari

Despite all the efforts that are being made towards women empowerment, the situation within the entrepreneurial space remains dire. Let’s look at the numbers:

  • Only 3 % of India’s GDP comes from women entrepreneurship
  • Just 2 % of all equity funds raised in 2017 went to women-led businesses
  • Women-led enterprises account for just 15 . 4 % of the 58.5 million establishments
  • About 343 million of Indian women who are of working age do not earn from formal, paid labour This goes to show how important it is to enable women entrepreneurs. When women own businesses, economies grow, employment rates rise and social trajectories change for the better.

The 5-point model of #EntrepreNaari

Guidance and Mentorship Offering expert advice and guidance from our extensive panel of mentors, which include entrepreneurs and domain experts from diverse fields.

Business Resources and Learning Opportunities From one-hour workshops to 15-day bootcamps, we have a vast range of learning modules that can help EntrepreNaaris stay ahead of the market, and build crucial skill sets across diverse functional areas.

Career Preview Providing a preview of their specific careers as well as the larger entrepreneurial world through our expert advisory sessions, which aims to synchronize skills into actions to foster a start-up ecosystem.

Peer Community Support Connecting EntrepreNaaris with other women who are all striving to build their business from ground up. They can chat with them, discuss their challenges, find solutions and enjoy peer support.

3E access to resources This is all about education, entrepreneurship and creating the capability to drive employment. That is why we have a curated directory of resources, from logistical companies to legal, technological and marketing experts, who can help women with every aspect of the business.

The EntrepreNaari community welcomes and engages entrepreneurs at various stages of their journey.

1) Want-erpreneurs:

They aspire to be entrepreneurs and have the big idea too, but at a very nascent stage. Here, they need to be guided to turn their 'wants' into 'haves'. They are eager to learn and absorb all the information, with large scale impact.

2) Early-stage entrepreneurs:

They are the ones who have trusted their vision and set up their business, but now want to expand. They have a sketch in their mind, which just needs polishing for greater results.

3) Mature entrepreneurs:

These are established entrepreneurs, who have found a foothold in the industry. With high-touch and low-technology approach, one can give them personal-customized guidance, leading to great potential for results. They are experienced leaders who just need a nudge in the right direction, and can also be backed as an investor.
